Intro ITWS

    • Atom template
  • Example:

  • Guidelines:
    • Create five items/entries, each linking to a news or blog posting, or any web content you find interesting (from three different web sites).
    • Use the same three items in both feeds.
    • Post the two files onto the "projects" page of your "iit" website.
    • The feeds must validate.
    • To look at the feeds in an RSS/Atom reader (found online) or browser. Note: View the feed in your browser after posting it to your website: most browsers have built in RSS and Atom readers, but the feed must be published on a website to be properly rendered.
    • Add a README.md file to your lab3 directory discussing this lab. Make sure to include a link to your projects page in the readme file.
    • Zip up your lab3 directory and upload it to the assignments area on LMS.
